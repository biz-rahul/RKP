My Favourite Movie of All Time

Lucy Shimmers and the Prince of Peace

2020

This is my favourite movie of all time — a deeply moving and spiritually rich story that stays with you long after the credits roll. Lucy Shimmers is a young girl whose innocent faith and pure heart create profound ripples in the lives of everyone she meets. The film beautifully captures the power of love, redemption, and grace in the most unexpected places. Every time I watch it, I discover something new — a moment, a line, a glance — that moves me all over again. It is not just a movie to me; it is a feeling, a reminder that even in our darkest hours, light finds a way through.
